Alexandre Neto

Geospatial Consultant

I work as geospatial consultant freelancer. I have more than 20 years of working experience. On my daily work, I help others with their GIS/Geospatial challenges and workflows, mainly using QGIS, GDAL, PostgreSQL/PostGIS, Python and other free and open source solutions.

I run regular online trainings on “Introduction to QGIS”, “Cartography and Datavis with QGIS”, “Relational Spatial Databases with QGIS and PostGIS”, and “Introduction to PyQGIS”.

I firmly believe that knowledge needs to be spread as much as possible. That is why I often share tips and tutorials on my blog, twitter, and Linkedin. I also answer questions on mailing lists, gis.stackexchange.com and the QGIS Community Telegram chat.

I try to be an active member of the QGIS community, where I help with documentation, testing, and Portuguese translations.

Services

Spatial Analysis

All types of spatial analysis, including, but not limited to: Overlay, multi-criteria, network, location-allocation, etc… Writing of scripts, using Python, PyQGIS, SQL or QGIS processing Modeller to automate analysis
Spatial Databases Management

Design, deploy and manage PostgreSQL/Postgis spatial relational databases, users and permissions, creation of triggers and other mechanisms for automation and ensurance of data consistency.
QGIS Plugins Development

Development of custom python plugins for QGIS. Creation of processing tools for using in longer workflows. Check the plugins section for examples.
Training and Technical Support

Online and presential trainings on Introduction to QGIS, Cartography and Datavis using QGIS, Spatial Relational Databases with PostGIS and QGIS etc… One-on-one coaching and technical support.
Cartography and WebGIS

Cartography, map design and production. Automation of multi-pages/ multi-thematic maps production. Installation and deployment of WebGIS systems for publish Maps/spatial data online.
Carttop/RECART support

Conversion of CAD to RECART databases (Portuguese Topographic Cartography). Creation of validation and fixing tools for QGIS. Technical support and coaching
